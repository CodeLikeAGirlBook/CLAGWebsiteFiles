Q. Where can I buy the book?

A. Yes! It's available at independent bookstores in your area, and online at Amazon and Target.

Q. Help!! I'm reading the book and there's something I don't get.

A. Coding gets complicated. I've got your back. I'll keep track of any issues that come up here, on Code Like a Girl's Github page, so head on over. If that doesn't work, send me a tweet.

Q. Where do I find a Raspberry Pi (for chapter 2) and those LED-wire-sensor thingies, I mean supplies?

A. Yeah, chapter 2 puts together a computer from a Raspberry Pi and chapter 5, creating code that makes sensors work and LEDs light up. You can just read along, but click here for ideas on how to find these parts.

Q. Miriam, why did Miriam write this book?

A. I didn't start off as a coder, only as someone who was fascinated by code -- and angry that the tech industry has become so male-dominated. That's why I decided to immerse in learning to code, and explain it to everyone else.
